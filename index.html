Details

WHAT

DevFest 2019 is a one-day hackathon where participants get together with friends or new people and create something awesome! This year for DevFest, the theme will be to build something using financial technology and/or using a Google service or technology.

WHERE

This year, we are happy to work with Northwestern Mutual, and DevFest will take place at their Cream City Labs innovation center. The address is:

805 E Mason St, Milwaukee, WI 53202

WHEN


Saturday
February 23rd, 2019
9am - 5pm

NM's Cream City Labs

For our first ever GDG DevFest, we're proud to be partnering with Northwestern Mutual and their Cream City Labs innovation space. NM has been a fantastic partner of UWM's College of Engineering and Sciences since their expansion in the Milwaukee area, and this will be a fantastic opportunity for all participants to network with NM staff and see what they're all about. CCL will set this hackathon apart from others due to the collaborative and high-tech nature of the space!

FAQ

Will lunch be provided? What about dietary restrictions?

We will be providing lunch in the form of Jimmy Johns subs! Vegetarian and gluten-free options will be provided based on sign-up survey :)

How do I get to Cream City Labs?

Cream City Labs is inside Northwestern Mutual's downtown HQ, please reference Google Maps to navigate.

Will transportation be provided?

Due to UWM restrictions on spending, we cannot provide transportation to the hackathon. We recommend carpooling with friends to save on emissions.

Is there parking available?

There is street parking in the area, and we are working with NM to see about getting parking at NM's building.

Who can attend?

GDG DevFest 2019 is open to all UWM students who are GDG members and over the age of 18, regardless of major or year. While technical majors are more common, non-tech majors can provide a lot of insight into particular projects! Sign up for GDG

How does judging work?

Judging will be done by Northwestern Mutual engineers and volunteer UWM faculty. Criteria is based on how much the project follows the theme, novelty, and execution of the idea.

Are there prizes for the winners?

We plan to give away Google Home and Chromecast products to the winners, and possibly other stuff from NM!

What about swag?

Yes! We will have t-shirts for every attendee and probably some other smaller swag items to give away!
